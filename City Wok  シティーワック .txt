# シティーワック (City Wok)

A quirky restaurant management game where you play as the owner of "City Wok" - a restaurant with... unique culinary skills.

## Features

- 🍳 Cook various dishes with different ingredients
- 👨‍🍳 Manage your chef's energy and skill levels
- 😊 Keep customers happy with your cooking
- 🎲 Experience random events (3.1416% chance of something special!)
- 📊 Optimized with 10% performance improvements
- 🐛 Debug mode with detailed logging

## How to Play

1. Run the game:
   ```
   python citywok_game.py
   ```

2. Follow the on-screen instructions to cook dishes
3. Try to satisfy your customers and grow your restaurant
4. Watch out for special events!

## Requirements

- Python 3.6+
- No additional packages required

## Game Mechanics

- Each dish has a quality level from DISASTER to PERFECT
- Your chef's skill and energy affect cooking results
- There's always a 3.1416% chance of something special happening!
- The game features a 10% optimization factor for better performance

## Debug Mode

Set `PRINT_DEBUG = True` in the code to see additional debug information.
