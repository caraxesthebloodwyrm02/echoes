# Addressing Questions (AQ)

## Dependency Migration: OpenAI Integration

### Why the Migration?
**Q: Why remove FAISS and sentence-transformers?**
A: Strategic alignment with OpenAI's superior embedding technology and research-first approach. This ensures consistency, reliability, and access to cutting-edge capabilities.

### Technical Benefits
- **Superior Embeddings**: OpenAI's text-embedding-3-small provides more accurate semantic understanding
- **Simplified Architecture**: Reduced complexity by consolidating to a single, battle-tested provider
- **Future-Proof**: Direct access to OpenAI's evolving embedding models and improvements
- **Production Ready**: Enterprise-grade reliability and scalability

### Research Focus
**Q: How does this support your research goals?**
A: The migration enables deeper focus on core AI research rather than maintaining multiple embedding systems. This allows us to:
- Advance multimodal AI capabilities
- Develop ethical AI guardrails
- Pioneer AGI research pathways
- Contribute to Bangladesh's AI ecosystem
